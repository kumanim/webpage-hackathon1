Credit Card

Convenient, handy, and useful – Credit Cards give you the freedom to make payments for your various expenses. Whatever your needs may be, you can swipe your NatWest Bank Credit Card and pay for the expenses.
Credit Cards are accepted as cash substitutes for all kinds of purchases – be it hotel or flight bookings, bill payments, purchasing the latest gadgets, and online and retail shopping. You can pay for small-value and big-ticket items easily with your NatWest Bank Credit Card.
What’s more, you can enjoy exclusive Credit Cards offers, discounts and reward points on your offline and online Credit Card purchases. Our tie-ups with top brands like Amazon, BookMyShow, Swiggy, Tata CliQ, among others on select cards, help you save more as you shop more

Saving Accounts

When one starts a banking journey, the most common type of account that can be opted for is a Savings Account. So why wait? It is high time you apply to open a Savings Account with NatWest Bank and kickstart your savings journey.
The idea behind a Savings Account is to encourage thrift or the habit of saving a portion of one's income. You can apply online to open a Savings Account with NatWest Bank and benefit from the wide range of features and exclusive deals that come along with the different variants. The online saving account opening process is simple and easy, and can be completed online in just a few steps.
*Regular Savings, Women's, SavingsMax, Senior Citizens & DigiSave Youth account can be opened digitally.
